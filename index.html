About

Technology has always fascinated me because of its ability to solve real-world problems. For me, software engineering is not just about writing code. It is about understanding challenges, designing thoughtful solutions, and building products that continue creating value long after deployment.

My experience extends beyond application development into systems administration, networking, DevOps, and cloud infrastructure. I enjoy understanding how every layer of a system works, from the user interface to the servers that keep it running. That broader perspective helps me build reliable, scalable solutions and take ownership of projects from concept to production.

I have had the opportunity to contribute to production software for organizations such as Refactory Academy and SwanAir Travels & Safaris, while also gaining hands-on experience in enterprise IT environments through systems administration and network support. Today, I am focused on building modern, maintainable software that solves meaningful business problems and supports long-term growth.
